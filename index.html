Overview

Configure costs and benefits, view NPV, ROI and BCR, and stress-test assumptions with scenario and sensitivity tools.

How it works

Enter baseline inputs, specify costs and benefits as annual streams, and the tool computes discounted results over the chosen horizon. Use sensitivity analysis to vary key parameters and export summaries for reporting.

Tip
Start with conservative assumptions, then explore optimistic and pessimistic scenarios.

NPV, BCR, ROI, payback period, plus downloadable tables.

Technical Appendix

Definitions, formulas, and assumptions used in the model.

Formulas

NPV is the sum over years of discounted net benefits.

BCR is total discounted benefits divided by total discounted costs.

ROI is discounted net benefits divided by discounted costs.
